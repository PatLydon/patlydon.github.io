Definition of cryptocurrency

They are electronic assets that are mobilized through cryptography in order to guarantee the reliability and ownership of monetary transactions. This encryption enables an exclusive system for the creation of future units, thus avoiding counterfeiting. There are 3 main aspects that define cryptocurrencies:

  • Blockchain: They use this autonomous database that records and examines the veracity of the exchanges made.
  • Without intermediaries: it is a decentralized network. Therefore, it does not require external agents to regulate its operations.
  • Without external backrest: no bank or financial institution is responsible for activities carried out with cryptocurrencies.

How it works BitIQ cryptocurrencies?

Cryptocurrencies are stored in Wallets or digital wallets that can work with the software or application format. These spaces provide the user with a password that grants him the right to operate with the assets.

Transactions are carried out between wallets using public codes (keys) that are responsible for transferring cryptocurrencies from one wallet to another. Subsequently, these movements are registered in the public ledger or chain of blocks (Blockchain).

Any user who has the same cryptocurrency, can enter the system and view the figures of the operations but not the information of the users. It is important to know that once the transaction is executed it is impossible to reverse it.
